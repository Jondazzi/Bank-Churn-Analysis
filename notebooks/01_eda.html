Complaints(0.996) have nearly a perfect correlation with customer churn, complaints will be the strongest predictor of a customer leaving the bank¶

Age(0.285) has a moderately positive correlation, older customers are more likely to church¶

IsActiveMember(-0.156) Active members are less likely Churn compared to inactive members¶

Balance(0.119) Customers with higher balances are slightly more likely to Churn¶

Features such as Point Earned, Satisfaction Score, HasCrCard, and Estimated Salary have negligible correlation and will not be prioritized in the analysis¶

Specifically Satisfaction Score(-0.006) nearly has no correlation with customer churn. Meaning the banks current method of evaluating customer satisfcation is not effectively capturing factors that cause customers to leave. So it is reccomended that the bank potentially reworks how it measures customer satisfaction so it can better retain customers¶
